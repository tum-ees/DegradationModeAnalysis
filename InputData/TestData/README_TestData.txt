Here, you find the pOCVs in both charge and discharge direction of a Molicel P45B cell cycled between 0 and 100% SOC for 800 equivalent full cycles.

The cell is one of the four cells used for the publication by Rehm et al. (linked to the DMA tool).

-> To use this TestData in the DMA tool, select s.inputIsAging_data_table = 1
